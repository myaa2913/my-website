Hi, I'm Matt.

I'm a people analytics leader at HubSpot, where I lead a data science team focused on employee productivity and developer experience. I build AI tools and employee-facing products that help people do their best work.

Before industry, I studied computational social science at the Stanford Graduate School of Business, where my research examined organizational culture, labor market inequality, and firm performance. My work has been published in the [*Harvard Business Review*](https://hbr.org/2020/01/the-new-analytics-of-culture) and [*Administrative Science Quarterly*](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/0001839219844175), among other places.

I write here occasionally about things I'm building, thinking about, or find interesting.
